Own the privileges of upcoming infrastructure that will make your future more convenient than ever at

Thane - Wadala Metro Line 4

  • It will be operated between Kasarvadavali at Ghodbunder Road, Thane to Wadala in Mumbai

  • A game changer to connect Mumbai with Thane.

  • The stretch of 32.32 km will cover 32 stations

Mumbai Metro Line 4A and Mumbai Metro Line 5

  • Metro Line 4A will be an extension of Metro Line 4 from Kasarvadavali to Gaikmukh area.

  • Metro Line 5 will connect Thane to Bhiwandi and Kalyan. The 24.9 km-long Metro corridor will have 17 stations.

Kopri Bridge widening Mumbai - Thane

  • The bridge will be widened from existing 4 Lanes to create an 8 lane bridge.

  • This project on Eastern Expressway Highway connects Mumbai and Thane.

Thane - Borivali underground tunnel

  • It will be 10.20-km underground tunnel passing through the Sanjay Gandhi National Park

  • It would take only around 15 minutes to commute between the 10.20 km stretch

  • It will start from Tikuji Ni Wadi (Thane) and connect Borivali at the western expressway

Water ways

  • It will connect Thane with Vasai, Kalyan, Mumbai and Navi Mumbai

  • Will save around one hour of travel time for commuters of Thane to Mumbai, while 30 minutes for commuters of Navi Mumbai

Thane - Ghodbunder elevated corridor

  • The 4.4 km elevated road will be a major connecter between NH-3 and Western Express Highway NH-8

  • The stretch will be from the Fountain Hotel on WEH to the Gaimukh area in the Sanjay Gandhi National Park (SGNP)

New Flyovers for easy commute

  • Commuters between Thane and Mumbai would be able to use three bridges as an alternative route to exit the city limits

  • Flyovers at LBS Marg near Castle Mill another at MG Road at Naupada and the third flyover Vandana Talkies, Thane are expected to ease the commuters’ problems
